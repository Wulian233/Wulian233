👋 Hi, I'm Wulian (literally means “covering face” in Chinese). I’m currently an 18-year-old high school student. My real name is **Jiahao Li**.

My main focus is on Minecraft Chinese localization and Python development.

All my programming work is driven by pure interest and passion. I’m a Minecraft mod developer with **over 6M+** total downloads across my projects.

I also serve as a co-lead of the [@VM-Chinese-translate-group](https://github.com/VM-Chinese-translate-group) , currently the largest non-profit Minecraft Chinese localization team, and I am the developer of its official website.
You can learn more about us [here](https://vmct-cn.top/).
